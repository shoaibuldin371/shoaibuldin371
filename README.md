### AI Powered E Commerce Chatbot

**Final Year Project**  
**Duration:** Oct 2025 to May 2026  
**Tech Stack:** Next.js, Python, NLP, Google Colab

An AI powered chatbot integrated into a custom online store. The chatbot helps customers search products using simple natural language queries such as:

> black shoes under 2000

### Project Highlights

- Developed an AI Powered E Commerce Chatbot integrated into a custom online store
- Built the chatbot to understand natural language customer queries and return relevant product results
- Worked on improving the online customer journey by making product search easier, faster, and more user friendly
- Created a custom NLP model trained on the store's product dataset using Google Colab
- Designed the model to identify product category, color, and price range from customer queries
- Supported product recommendation logic to help users discover suitable products more easily
